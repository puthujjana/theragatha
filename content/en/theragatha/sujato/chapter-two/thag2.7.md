## 2.7 Rādha  

Just as rain seeps into  
A poorly roofed house,  
Lust seeps into  
An undeveloped mind.  

Just as rain doesn’t seep into  
A well roofed house,  
Lust doesn’t seep into  
A well-developed mind.
