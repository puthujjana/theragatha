## 2.22 Bhaddiya Kāligodhāputta  

“Ah, happiness! Ah, happiness!”—thus they call me.  
But I wasn’t unhappy before, nor am I unhappy now.  

Before, I had what I wanted,  
Now I have no desire for anything.
