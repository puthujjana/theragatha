## 2.26 Uttiya  

Go beyond being clever,  
And give up all cleverness;  
Don’t get caught up in conceit,  
But train yourself in peace.  

Go beyond the pathless jungle,  
The bondage of Māra.  
Be without attachments,  
And realize the supreme peace.
