## 2.48 Ekavihāriya  

In the wilderness I dwell alone,  
Peaceful, free from fear;  
No noise disturbs my meditation—  
I live in happiness.  

Having crossed the flood,  
I’ve reached the far shore;  
Without taints, I’m quenched,  
Truly at peace.
