## 2.27 Devasabha  

Speak only after considering  
The words you are about to say;  
Don’t speak lies,  
And don’t say what will lead to harm.  

When you speak the truth,  
Say what’s beneficial,  
And don’t hurt anyone—  
That’s the Dhamma of the Buddhas.
