## 2.25 Abhaya  

Whoever undertakes the holy life,  
Intending to find peace of mind,  
Should be scrupulous and restrained,  
Mindful and fully aware.  

When you go out,  
When you come back,  
When you look ahead and aside,  
Restrain yourself in all situations.
