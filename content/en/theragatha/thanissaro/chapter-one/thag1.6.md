# 1.6 Dabba (“Capable”)

Whoever,  
hard to tame,  
has been tamed by taming,  
—capable, contented,  
crossed over doubt,  
victorious, as his fears  
are dispersed:  
He is capable,  
unbound,  
steadfast in mind.
