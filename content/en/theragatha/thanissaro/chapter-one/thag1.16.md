# 1.16 Belaṭṭhasīsa

Just as a fine thoroughbred steed,  
with swishing tail & mane  
runs with next-to-no effort,  
so my days & nights  
run with next-to-no effort  
now that I’ve gained a pleasure  
not of the flesh.[^1]  

## Notes

[^1]: See SN 36:31 and Thag 1:85.
