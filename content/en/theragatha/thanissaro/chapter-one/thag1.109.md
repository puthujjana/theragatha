# 1.109 Saṅgharakkhita

Why,  
having gone into solitude,  
does he not take account  
of the message  
of the one sympathetic  
to his foremost well-being?  
Because he dwells  
with his faculties  
exposed,  
like a tender deer  
in the forest.
