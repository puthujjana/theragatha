# 1.21 Nigrodhatthera

“Nāhaṁ bhayassa bhāyāmi,  
Satthā no amatassa kovido;  
Yattha bhayaṁ nāvatiṭṭhati,  
Tena maggena vajanti bhikkhavo”ti.
