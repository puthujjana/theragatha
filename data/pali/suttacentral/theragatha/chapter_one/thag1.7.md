# 1.7 Bhalliya

“Yopānudī maccurājassa senaṁ,
Naḷasetuṁva sudubbalaṁ mahogho;
Vijitāvī apetabheravo hi,
Danto so parinibbuto ṭhitatto”ti.

Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā bhalliyo thero gāthaṁ abhāsitthāti.
