# 1.19 Kulatthera

“Udakañhi nayanti nettikā,  
Usukārā namayanti tejanaṁ;  
Dāruṁ namayanti tacchakā,  
Attānaṁ damayanti subbatā”ti.  

## Attribution

… Kulo thero ….  

## Notes

Variant: Kulattheragāthā → kuṇḍalattheragāthā (bj)

Variant: namayanti → damayanti (mr)
